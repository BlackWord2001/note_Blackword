+ 是的 我也这么认为 <br>
Yeah. I think so too.

+ 别感到抱歉<br>
don't be sorry.

+ 说我不可爱是不是有些苛刻<br>
I'm not cute isn't that a bit harsh

+ 非常可爱<br>
is extremely cute

+ 对不起，不小心说出来了<br>
I'm sorry, It just came out!!

+ 总是这样，对不起你受伤了吗？<br>
as always, I'm sorry are you hurt anywhere?

+ 别老给我添麻烦啊<br>

you chose now to say that?

you're not hurt are you?
